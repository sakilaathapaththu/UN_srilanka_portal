Human Rights Sri Lanka Office
About Us

Upholding Human Rights & Human Dignity in Sri Lanka

The United Nations Human Rights Organization – Sri Lanka works with communities, civil society and state institutions to promote and protect the fundamental rights of every person in the country. We support dialogue, accountability and inclusive development so no one is left behind.

Monitoring & Reporting

Documenting human rights situations and sharing evidence-based reports with national and international partners.

Community Engagement

Working with local communities, youth and vulnerable groups through outreach and capacity-building.

Support & Referral

Providing guidance, information and referrals for individuals affected by discrimination and abuse.

Policy & Advocacy

Supporting reforms that strengthen rule of law, equality and access to justice for all.

Our Values
Dignity

Every person deserves respect, safety and equal protection of the law.

Equality

We stand against discrimination in all its forms across Sri Lanka.

Justice

We promote accountability and fair access to remedies and redress.
